Our Story

Born from a belief that news should be accessible to everyone

Xpress News was founded in 2022 with a simple but powerful idea: that quality journalism should not be locked behind paywalls or buried under clickbait. We set out to build a news platform that respects its readers — delivering accurate, well-reported stories without the noise.

What started as a small team of passionate journalists and developers has grown into one of the region's most-read digital news outlets, covering everything from global geopolitics and business to health, technology, sports and education.

We believe an informed public is the foundation of a healthy society. That belief drives everything we do — from the stories we choose to cover to the care we take in how we present them.

Our Mission

To inform, to challenge, to connect

Our mission is to provide fast, accurate and fair reporting on the issues that shape our world. We hold power to account, amplify underreported voices and give our readers the context they need to understand a complex world.

⚡

Speed Without Shortcuts

We get stories to our readers fast — but never at the expense of accuracy. Every fact is checked before it is published.
